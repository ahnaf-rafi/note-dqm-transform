\begin{abstract}
This note revisits a result on preservation of differentiability in quadratic
mean under measurable transformations.
The goal is to provide clarity about some of the technical machinery behind the
result at the expense of lengthier exposition than can be found in some
classical texts, e.g.
\citet[Theorem 7.2, p. 70]{1981ibragimovStatisticalEstimation} and
\citet[Proposition A.5.5, p. 461]{1998bickelEfficientAdaptiveEstimation},
amongst others.
\end{abstract}
